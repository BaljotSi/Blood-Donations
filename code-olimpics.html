Blood Donations

importance of donating Blood

Blood is essential to help patients survive surgeries, cancer treatment, chronic illnesses, and traumatic injuries.

Doubts

The side effects of donating blood include nausea and dizziness and fainting in some cases.

Facts

More than 38,000 blood donations are needed every day.

Procedure

We will insert a needle in your arm which will collect your blood into a blood bag with your unique donor number.You should not feel any discomfort or pain.
